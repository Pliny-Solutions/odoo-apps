HR Training and Development Programs

HR Training and Development Programs helps organizations manage, track, and improve employee training initiatives. The app allows HR teams to create training programs, assign employees, monitor participation, and record outcomes. With smart views inside the Employee module, managers can easily see the trainings each employee has attended, ensuring continuous development, compliance, and alignment with organizational goals.
